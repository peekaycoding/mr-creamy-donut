## 🍩 How to Order

1. **Browse Products** – Go to the [Menu](https://peekaycoding.github.io/mr-creamy-donut/menu.html) page
2. **Add to Cart** – Select your favorite donuts and adjust quantities
3. **Checkout** – Click the cart icon and proceed to checkout
4. **Enter Details** – Fill in your shipping information
5. **Choose Payment** – Select your preferred payment method
   - **EFT** – Pay via bank transfer and send proof via WhatsApp/Email
   - **Cash** – Pay on delivery or when picking up
   - **PayPal** – Pay securely with PayPal.Me
6. **Confirm Order** – Review your details and confirm
7. **Get Receipt** – Download or print your order receipt
