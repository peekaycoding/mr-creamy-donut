Our Story

From a small bakery to your favorite donut shop

Welcome to Mr. Creamy Donut

It all started with a young boy named Pakiso who grew up enjoying donuts. Fast forward he moved to vanderbijlpark in 2023 then began to enjoy the donuts more at the local bakery in Vanderbijlpark. His favorite was always the simple glazed ring, but he dreamed of creating something even more special.

In 2025 as he grew older Pakiso his passion for donuts only intensified. He began experimenting in his resident kitchen, trying to recreate the perfect donut. After many attempts (and some delicious failures), he finally created what would become our signature donut - the Creamy Chocolate Bliss.

At first, Pakiso just made donuts for himself. But when he shared them with friends and resident mates, they couldn't get enough. Word spread quickly through Vanderbijlpark and soon people were asking to buy his creations.

From Kitchen to Shop

What started as a small resident operation quickly grew. Pakiso began selling his donuts at local markets and events, always with lines of eager customers. The demand became so great that in 2025, he opened the first MR. CREAMY DONUT shop in the heart of Vanderbijlpark.

Today, we still use Pakiso's original recipes and techniques, but we've expanded our menu to include a variety of delicious donuts that cater to all tastes. From the classic glazed ring to our famous Creamy Chocolate Bliss, each donut is made with the same love and attention to detail that Pakiso put into his very first batch.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable donut experiences by combining traditional craftsmanship with innovative flavors, using only premium ingredients sourced from trusted local suppliers. Every donut we make is a tribute to Pakiso's original vision - to create the most delicious, creamy, satisfying donuts Vanderbijlpark has ever tasted.

We're proud to be part of the Vanderbijlpark and Bedworthpark communities, and we look forward to serving you our delicious donuts for many years to come!
